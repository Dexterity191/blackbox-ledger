# Privacy Policy — Blackbox Ledger

**Last updated:** May 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how **Blackbox Ledger** (the "Application"), an internal application operated by **Blackbox Research** (the "Organization"), a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, handles data.

The Application is for the Organization's internal use only. It is not made available to the public.

## 1. Information We Access

The Application accesses the following data on behalf of the Organization through authorized API integrations with Intuit (QuickBooks Online) and Ramp:

- **Financial data** from QuickBooks Online, including chart of accounts, transactions, invoices, bills, payments, journal entries, vendors, customers, classes, departments, and financial reports.
- **Spend and card data** from Ramp, including transactions, receipts, reimbursements, bills, vendors, departments, locations, users, and statements.
- **OAuth credentials** (Client ID, Client Secret, refresh tokens, realm identifiers) required to authenticate API access.

No data is collected from any party outside the Organization.

## 2. How We Use Information

Data accessed by the Application is used exclusively for the Organization's internal purposes, including:

- Automated accounting workflows and reconciliation
- Financial reporting and analysis
- Audit preparation
- Operational and compliance reviews

## 3. Data Storage and Security

- The Application is self-hosted on infrastructure controlled by the Organization.
- OAuth credentials and tokens are stored locally in environment files with restricted file-system permissions and are not transmitted to any third party other than Intuit and Ramp.
- Access to the Application and its credentials is limited to authorized administrators of the Organization.

## 4. Data Sharing

The Organization does not sell, rent, share, or otherwise disclose data accessed via the Application to any third party, except:

- To the underlying API providers (Intuit, Ramp) as necessary to perform requested operations
- As required by law, court order, or regulatory request
- To the Organization's auditors, accountants, or legal advisors under appropriate confidentiality obligations

## 5. Data Retention

Data accessed by the Application is retained only as long as necessary for internal accounting purposes and in accordance with the Organization's records-retention policies and applicable legal requirements (including IRS recordkeeping requirements for 501(c)(3) organizations).

## 6. Third-Party Services

The Application relies on the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

- **Intuit (QuickBooks Online):** https://www.intuit.com/privacy/
- **Ramp:** https://ramp.com/legal/privacy-policy

## 7. Changes to This Policy

The Organization may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
